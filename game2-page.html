Description

The year is 2032. Humanity is actively exploring space and looking for new ways to improve the race. You, as part of an experimental group, have been sent to lunar orbit and are a participant in an unusual project. But, as usual, something went wrong and instead of spinning in front of the scientists as an experimental hamster, you are forced to fight for your own life. The plot is exciting, but only those who are ready to buy Prey will be able to get to the station captured by aliens.

You have to uncover many secrets, including not only the dark history of "Talos-1", but also events from your past. Try to survive in the spinning meat grinder. Feeling like a game is a controversial pleasure, but you have every chance of surviving if you manage not to panic. Use your cunning mind and golden hands to the full, capable of transforming any of the available means into weapons. Clear the ship from evil spirits and return to your home as a winner!

Game features

  • One world - one location. inear play replaced the relatively open world, limited by the space station. The location is really the only one, but it is permissible to wool it up and down.

  • Character. While inventing your hero, do not get hung up on the image of an unshaven stern man, perhaps with a pretty girl passing the obstacle course in the form of difficult levels will be more exciting.

  • Challenge or fun? Difficulty setting is now your prerogative. Adjust the parameters that control movement, the way of confronting enemies and make the gameplay hellish, exhausting to emotional exhaustion test - or, conversely, a pleasant walk through the levels.

  • An unprecedented enemy. The alien creatures that populate Tallos 1 are perfect in their cunning. Ruthless predators systematically destroy team members. It is not easy to stop them, but you have no other way to stay alive, so turn on your brain and take the fight!

  • Make your game. Hurry up to buy the Prey key to open the hunt for intruders. Get and pump over superpowers. Explore the surrounding space and use everything that comes to hand to create new weapons and useful items. Improvise, improvise and improvise again! It is a non-standard approach to confronting an unprecedented threat that will become the key to the success of the entire enterprise.
