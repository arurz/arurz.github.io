Description

The iconic adventure of the Guardian Princess of Hallownest continues! Hornet again fights evil, while making a pilgrimage ascent to the top of the kingdom. She is still fragile, tender, open and always ready for new challenges!

Finding the truth and bringing peace back to new lands will not be easy. But are you ready to buy Hollow Knight and come to the aid of the beautiful warrior?

Game features

  • The splendor of a suffering world. Visit exquisitely detailed gilded cities, walk along fiery lakes and through foggy swamps. Hand-drawn 2D rendering of a vast, interconnected and open world to explore is truly gorgeous!

  • New enemies. Who will not try to stop the graceful Hornet on the way to the goal! She will have to confront kings and assassins, predators and hunters, monsters and "noble" knights. More than one and a half hundred enemies, and all this for one fragile warrior princess!

  • Fatal acrobatics. Learn new moves and link them into killer combos. Use the mesmerizing dance of death in battles with enemies and win!

  • Renewable weapons. The arsenal of weapons will be regularly updated with new items. Explore the possibilities of traps and mechanisms in order to use them with maximum efficiency against enemies.

  • Shocking missions. Fulfill the desires of the enslaved: catch unique animals, uncover ancient secrets, search for treasures lost in centuries. Don't let them completely lose faith in life and get ready for unexpected turns of events.

  • Control in the head. Having successfully completed the main mission, do not rush to rest on your laurels. Put your skills to the test in the crazy Silk Soul mode. This bomb will allow you to look at the path traveled from a completely different angle!
