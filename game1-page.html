Description

We sing glory to the madness of the world! And he really lost his mind, so much so that William Gibson's fantasies are resting. Moral values ​​have lost their relevance to people. Digital drugs and the cyberization of the body are in vogue. The former make it possible to live with someone else's emotions, both legal:

  • happiness
  • orgasm
  • euphoria
  • not quite

    • The latter turn people into complete robots that can be taken under control and used for any purpose.

    The once pretty town of Night City has turned into a nest of debauchery and poverty. It is ruled by drug dealers, black cyber-transplant specialists, gangsters. Do you want to know how ordinary people survive in this cyber-dungeon and try to change the situation for the better? Then hurry up to buy Cyberpunk 2077. The end of the world is just around the corner!

    Game features

  • Character editor. You can customize the hero in all respects, choosing his gender, appearance and even profession. The further development of events will directly depend on who the protagonist becomes - a journalist, policeman, hacker, scientist or just a street tramp (there are 9 roles in total).

  • They are greeted by clothes. The appearance of the character is important, since it is he who influences the building of communications in the game. If you want the meeting to go smoothly, dress appropriately for the occasion. Fill your wardrobe with a variety of items, from a bunch of bucks business suit to Mad Max-style rags. Believe me, you will find all this very useful!

  • Non-linear gameplay. Which way to go through quests and missions is up to you. But before making your choice, remember that the consequences of the steps taken now will certainly come back to haunt in the future! The lack of a clear gradation of good and evil will make you sweat over the search for a way out of every unpleasant situation.

  • Total augmentation. Cybernetic modification of body parts has moved from the category of necessary procedures to mandatory ones. But even though sophisticated implants have become available more than ever, still try to preserve the remnants of humanity. In pursuit of a stylish look, emphasize the practicality of the artificial part. Whether to install a chrome-plated hand prosthesis or not is a matter of taste, but the translation chip will definitely come in handy. And then how will it go...

  • A squad of psychos. Join the brave team of guys who catch the dumb inadequate. Pick up your comrades-in-arms in the right cause and try to put things in order on the city streets!
